How our love blossomed

Our Love Story

Everything started with a greeting. From strangers, we became friends, and then our hearts beat as one. Through ups and downs, we have shared so much joy, laughter, and grown together every day.

  • First Meeting

    September 30, 2023

    Son and Linh had known each other before through an English class, but their first real meeting was during a backpacking trip to Ha Giang. Joining the same travel group, they finally had the chance to talk and get to know each other better while exploring the breathtaking landscapes together. Through shared adventures and laughter on winding mountain roads, their friendship quickly blossomed into something truly special.

  • Officially Dating

    Some time later

    As time passed, Son and Mai Linh became closer. Their weekends were filled with adventures, trying new foods, and deep conversations. Son admired Linh's gentleness and intelligence, while Linh loved Son's enthusiasm and energy. They became inseparable, always together through challenges and enjoying happiness.

  • Engagement

    June 28, 2025

    After years together, Son and Mai Linh realized they wanted to spend the rest of their lives with each other. They decided to take the next step and plan their wedding, marking a new and meaningful chapter in their journey together.
